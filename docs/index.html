Installation

Ready in 2 steps

1

Download & open

Download the DMG from the link above, open it, and run OneUp.app.

2

Bypass Gatekeeper

Because OneUp is not yet notarized, macOS will block the first launch. Try to open the app, then go to System Settings → Privacy & Security, scroll down, and click "Open Anyway". The Go Up button appears in your Finder toolbar automatically. Notarization requires an Apple Developer account — which costs $99/year.
